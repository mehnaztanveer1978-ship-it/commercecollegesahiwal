1. General Information & Mission

History & Campuses

  • Established in **1964** as Government Commercial Training Institute.
  • Upgraded to B.Com in 1986 and **Postgraduate (M.Com) in 2003**.
  • **Old Campus:** Liaquat Road (Ph: 040 9200401-02).
  • **New Campus:** Jahaz Ground (Ph: 040 4220401), features Hostel and Postgraduate Block.
  • Affiliated with BISE Sahiwal, PBTE Lahore, and **GCUF Faisalabad** for BS Programmes.

Our Mission

"To impart Quality Commerce/Business/Computer Education to prepare students for the challenges of their future practical and business life, focusing on their spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical development."

Inspirational Message

"You must concentrate on gaining knowledge and education. It is your foremost responsibility... Education is a matter of life and death to our nation."

— Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

2. Academic Programs

Intermediate Programs (I.Com & ICS)

I.Com (Commerce)

Total Marks: 1200

  • Principles of Accounting I & II
  • Principles of Commerce
  • Business Mathematics & Statistics

ICS (CS, Maths, Eco)

Total Marks: 1200

  • Computer Science I & II
  • Mathematics I & II
  • Principles of Economics I & II

ICS (CS, Stats, Eco / Maths)

Total Marks: 1200

Available with either Statistics or Mathematics combination.

Degree Programs (ADP & BS)

ADP-Commerce

2-Year Program (4 Semesters)

Focuses on core commerce subjects: Accounting, Business Law, Finance, and Auditing.

BS-Commerce

4-Year Program (8 Semesters)

Includes advanced studies in Strategic Management, Business Taxation, and E-commerce.

BS-Accounting & Finance

4-Year Program (8 Semesters)

Specialized program covering Corporate Finance, Investment Analysis, and Financial Modeling.

3. Admissions & Eligibility

Key Eligibility Criteria

  • ✓ **Merit-based admission** for all programs.
  • ✓ For **Intermediate**: Must have passed Matriculation (Science required for ICS).
  • ✓ For **ADP/BS**: Must have Intermediate/Equivalent and **Domicile of Punjab**.
  • ✓ Age Limit: **20 Years** (Principal can grant 4-year relaxation).

Reserved Quotas

3% quota for Disables.

5% quota for Minorities.

20 Marks awarded to Hafiz-e-Quran candidates.

Required Documents

Bring **Original Documents** and 4 attested photocopies.

  • Result Card of last attended class.
  • Character Certificate.
  • CNIC (Self/Father/Guardian) or B.Form.
  • Domicile Certificate (Punjab only).

4. Rules & Conduct

Uniform & Timings

  • **Mandatory Uniform** for all students.
  • **Boys:** Grey Trousers, White/Light Blue Shirt.
  • **Girls:** White Shalwar Kameez (Full Sleeve).
  • Timings: Starts at **8:00/8:30 a.m.** (Summer/Winter).

Attendance & Exams

  • **80% attendance** required (75% for ADP/BS).
  • Monthly **Class Tests** are mandatory.
  • Less than **50% in Send-up Tests** results in not being allowed for Annual Exam.
  • Continuous **6 days absence** without permission results in being struck off the roll.

Strict Disciplinary Rules

  • **Mobile Phones are Not Allowed** (Fine: Rs. 1000/-).
  • **Student Unionism** & Political Activities are strictly prohibited.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited (Fine: Rs. 500/-).
  • All communications must be routed through the **Principal**.
